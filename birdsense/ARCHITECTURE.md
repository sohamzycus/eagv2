### What is SAM-Audio?

SAM-Audio is Meta's state-of-the-art audio separation model that enables:
- **Zero-shot source separation** using text, audio, or point prompts
- **High-quality isolation** of bird calls from complex soundscapes
- **Multi-source handling** for chorus/multi-bird scenarios

| Challenge | Traditional Approach | With SAM-Audio |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------|
| **Noisy recordings** | Spectral subtraction (artifacts) | Clean separation (no artifacts) |

| **Multi-bird chorus** | Cannot separate | Individual bird extraction |
| **Urban noise** | Bandpass (loses detail) | Semantic separation |
| **Wind/rain** | Difficult to remove | Easy with text prompt |
